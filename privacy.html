Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Summary: Flura is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Do Not Collect

Flura does not collect any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device unique identifiers
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics data
  • Crash reports

1.2 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your local device:

  • Time block records (start time, end time, labels, categories)
  • Task lists
  • Goal settings
  • Preference settings (theme selection, reminder settings, etc.)
  • User nickname and avatar (optional)

2. Data Storage and Sync

2.1 Local Storage

All application data is stored locally on your iPhone by default, using iOS system's encrypted storage mechanism (SQLite database in app sandbox).

2.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Data will sync to your personal iCloud account
  • Sync is handled by Apple's CloudKit service
  • We cannot access your iCloud data
  • Data transmission uses end-to-end encryption
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Apple Services

Flura uses the following Apple services:

  • StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases (subscriptions)
  • CloudKit: Optional iCloud data synchronization

These services are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

3.2 Advertising Service (Free Version Only)

The free version uses Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • AdMob may collect device information and ad interaction data
  • We do not provide any of your personal information to AdMob
  • AdMob cannot access your time records, tasks, or notes
  • AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Subscribe to Flura Pro to remove all ads

4. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Display your time records and statistics within the app
  • Generate daily/weekly reports (Pro subscription required)
  • Sync data across your devices (if iCloud is enabled)

We will never:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use it for advertising targeting
  • Share it with any third parties (unless required by law)

5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Use iOS system's sandbox mechanism to isolate app data
  • Local data uses iOS file encryption
  • iCloud sync uses end-to-end encryption
  • No central server exists, eliminating data breach risks

6. Children's Privacy

Flura is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you discover that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us via App Store reviews, and we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is visible within the app
  • Export: Export data in CSV/JSON format
  • Delete: Clear all data in settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date on this page

Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please leave a review on the App Store. We read every review and continuously improve based on your feedback.
